Falmouth Exeter Plus use a variety of technologies to communicate and collaborate with and in support of students.

  • Slack.com allows ongoing private discussion within the organisation
  • youcanbook.me for appointment booking
  • knowdium provides forums that are accessible across HE institutions
  • loomio facilitates collaborative decision making
  • Twitter allows for communication of events and open public dialogue with students/staff and beyond

If you work for Falmouth Exeter Plus and would like access to any of these services contact your technologist
